About Community Mangrove Watch

Empowering communities to protect our coasts through technology and collective action

Our Mission

To bridge the critical data gap in mangrove conservation by empowering coastal communities with technology that transforms passive observers into active environmental guardians.

We believe that those closest to the problem are best positioned to be part of the solution. Our platform creates a powerful feedback loop where local insights directly inform and accelerate official conservation action.

"Our goal is to create a powerful feedback loop where local insights directly inform and accelerate official action, transforming passive observers into active guardians of their environment."

Our Impact Vision

Transforming conservation through community empowerment and technology

Enhanced Surveillance

Transforming limited official oversight into comprehensive, community-driven monitoring that covers vast coastal areas with real-time incident detection and reporting.

  • 24/7 community monitoring network
  • Geographic coverage expansion
  • Early threat detection

Community Empowerment

Providing tools for active environmental participation, fostering a sense of ownership and responsibility that creates lasting positive change in coastal communities.

  • Active citizen participation
  • Environmental stewardship culture
  • Local knowledge integration

Data-Driven Action

Equipping authorities with precise, timely data for effective intervention, resource allocation, and evidence-based policy making for sustainable mangrove management.

  • Real-time decision support
  • Evidence-based enforcement
  • Resource optimization

Biodiversity Preservation

Direct contribution to protecting critical marine habitats and species through rapid threat response and community-based conservation initiatives.

  • Habitat protection
  • Species conservation
  • Ecosystem health monitoring
