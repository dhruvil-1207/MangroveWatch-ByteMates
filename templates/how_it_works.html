How It Works

From community report to official action - our streamlined workflow ensures every submission contributes to conservation

1

REPORT

Community Members Submit Incidents

Citizens, fishermen, and coastal residents easily report environmental incidents directly from their mobile devices using our user-friendly web platform. No app installation required - just open your browser and start protecting your environment.

  • Geotagged photos with location data
  • Detailed incident descriptions
  • Mobile-optimized interface
  • Real-time submission
2

VALIDATE

AI-Powered Verification

Our advanced system leverages AI and satellite imagery to automatically verify the accuracy and severity of submitted reports, ensuring data integrity and reducing false positives while maintaining rapid processing times.

  • Machine learning validation algorithms
  • Satellite imagery cross-reference
  • Automated severity assessment
  • False positive filtering
3

ALERT

Instant Authority Notification

Verified incidents instantly appear on our secure, centralized dashboard accessible to relevant government agencies, conservation NGOs, and environmental authorities, ensuring rapid awareness and response coordination.

  • Real-time dashboard updates
  • Secure authority access
  • Automated notifications
  • Geographic incident mapping
4

ACT

Official Response & Enforcement

Government agencies and conservation organizations utilize this real-time, actionable data to deploy resources effectively, conduct thorough investigations, and implement appropriate enforcement measures to protect mangrove ecosystems.

  • Resource deployment coordination
  • Enforcement action initiation
  • On-site investigation
  • Impact assessment and monitoring
